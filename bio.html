Steph Xu

Steph is currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and Biotechnology at Tufts University. She is now a computational chemistry research assistant in the Tufts Lin's Lab. There, she is working on the Cyclic Peptide Project funded by NIH and NIGMS, which aims to utilize computational methods towards understanding and designing cyclic peptides, an important biological structure that affects protein-protein interactions in human bodies. Apart from academia, Steph spends her precious free time watching stand-up comedies and being a freelance writer for WeChat food channels.
